Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

This site (ahmedmaher.dev) is the personal portfolio of Ahmed Maher. It uses two privacy-respecting analytics tools to understand how visitors use the site so it can be improved. No accounts, logins, or personal profiles are required to browse, and the site does not sell your data or run advertising.

Who to contact

Data controller: Ahmed Maher — Ahclever2006@gmail.com.

Google Analytics 4

Collects anonymous usage data (pages viewed, sections reached, buttons clicked, approximate location, device/browser, referrer). IP addresses are anonymized by default and are not stored. Uses cookies such as _ga. Data retention is set to a maximum of 14 months.

More: Google Privacy Policy · How Analytics safeguards data

Microsoft Clarity

We partner with Microsoft Clarity to capture how you use and interact with this website through behavioral metrics, heatmaps, and (where technically possible) session replay, to improve the site. Usage data is captured using first- and third-party cookies such as _clck and _clsk. Sensitive input is masked and not recorded.

More: Microsoft Privacy Statement

Cookies & your choices

These analytics cookies are non-essential. You can block or delete them at any time through your browser settings, or by using a browser extension or privacy mode — the site remains fully functional without them. For visitors in the EU/EEA, UK, and Switzerland, Microsoft Clarity withholds cookies and data collection unless a valid consent signal is present.
